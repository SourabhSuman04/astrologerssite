Astrology

What is Astrology?

Astrology is not magic, but a scientific and spiritual discipline that studies the movements of celestial bodies – the Sun, Moon, planets, and constellations – and their influence on human life. Through astrology, one can understand future possibilities, auspicious timings, eclipses, seasonal changes, and planetary alignments.

Importance of Panchang

The Panchang not only highlights festivals, rituals, and auspicious moments but also provides insights into planetary transits, sunrise-sunset timings, and significant astrological events that influence our lives.

Astrology in Modern Life

Even today, astrology acts as a guiding light, helping individuals make confident decisions and progress in their spiritual and personal journey. It is a timeless tool to align with cosmic energy and bring clarity to life.
