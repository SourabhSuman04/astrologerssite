What is Premarital Counseling

Premarital counseling means preparing the boy and girl mentally, physically and emotionally for the upcoming marriage.

Why necessary

Before marriage, the boy - girl may have met each other, you know, have been together, but as soon as the marriage takes place, the responsibility of changing roles, which one has to understand and live up to the expectations of the other. Must get off Counseling prepares you for this.

Why was it not necessary before

Earlier marriages were mostly arranged. Due to not being financially self-dependent, she used to get married.

How does advice work

Pre-marriage counseling determines your mental and emotional maturity, likes and dislikes, whether you are capable of carrying on the relationship at that time. Counselors from the views of two people decide whether they will be happy with each other. According to this, the counselor answers the question, then the result comes out.

Remember

Tolerance, orderliness, the determination to maintain relationships and positive thinking is what makes a marriage successful. Therefore, you must have pre marriage counseling.
