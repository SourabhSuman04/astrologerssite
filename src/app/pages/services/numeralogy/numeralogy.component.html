Vedic Numerology is one of the most popular systems of Numerology. It is based on an ancient Indian system that applies special meanings to numbers and their symbols.

This is why Vedic numerology and other forms of Eastern numerology are so accurate. They recognize that numbers are everywhere in life and their vibrations deeply affect us on both a conscious and unconscious level.

Numbers 1-9 have specific meanings in the Vedas. This is where the basic foundation of Vedic numerology began. Over time, more patterns were found in the numbers 1-9 and new theories about them were applied in Vedic numerology.

Number is also a certain vibration, a certain energy. Numbers are the key to understanding human behaviour, they help to reach deeper layers of human personality.

The number helps to determine the personality of a person, his talents, behaviour, hidden obstacles, inner needs.

Moolank (मूलांक)

Our psychic type is associated with the date, time and place of birth. It is calculated by summing the digits of the date of birth into a single digit. Example: Born on 21 → 2+1 = 3.

Bhagyank (भाग्यांक)

Bhagyank shows what we are destined to become. It is calculated using the full date of birth (day, month, year). Example: 11.07.1975 → 1+1+0+7+1+9+7+5 = 31 → 3+1 = 4.

Name Figure

Every letter corresponds to a number. The name number is calculated by summing the values of all letters. Example: Kailash → 2+1+1+3+1+3+5 = 16 → 1+6 = 7.
